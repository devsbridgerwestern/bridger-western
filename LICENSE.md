## 🔧 Common Issues & Fixes

**❓ GUI doesn't appear after executing the script**
> Press **`RightShift`** (default toggle hotkey) to show or hide the panel. If it still doesn't show, fully detach and re-attach your executor after loading completely into the game — not from the Roblox loading screen.

**❓ Auto Fish isn't triggering the minigame**
> Make sure your character is standing at a valid fishing spot and that your rod is equipped. Auto Fish requires a fishing rod in the active slot to detect the cast prompt. If a rod is equipped and it still isn't working, toggle the module off and back on.

**❓ Insta Kill isn't working on certain enemies**
> Some boss-tier NPCs and event enemies in Bridger Western have server-validated health systems that resist direct manipulation. Insta Kill works on standard wildlife and regular NPCs — scripted boss fights may require manual combat.

**❓ Auto Farm stops collecting after a few cycles**
> The farm loop can lose its node reference when the server refreshes resource positions. Toggle **Auto Farm** off and back on to reset the collection path cleanly.

**❓ Speed Boost causes rubber-banding**
> Reduce the speed multiplier in the GUI slider. Values above 5x–6x tend to exceed the server's position validation tolerance and trigger rollback. Use 4x for the best balance between speed and stability.

**❓ Script throws an error on Android executor**
> Ensure your mobile executor (Delta, Arceus X, Fluxus) is updated to its latest version. Older builds may fail to parse the GUI library correctly. Also confirm you are executing *after* fully loading into a Bridger Western server.

**❓ Teleport Hub sends me to incorrect locations**
